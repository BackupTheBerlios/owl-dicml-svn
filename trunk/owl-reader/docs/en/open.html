Open a dictionary

Click on »Menu, Open dictionary«.

This will open a dialog where you can choose the file of the dictionary you want to open. Click on »Open« after that.

Maybe you are asked wether you want to index the dictionary. That has to happen at the first use. Click on »Yes«.

hint: If you get error-messages when opening a dictionary or when you installed a new version of an existing one use »Options, Force indexing« to re-index the dictionary.

Now choose an entry from the list or type the searched word into the text-box. When opening the first dictionary after starting owl it may take some seconds until the entry is shown.

If you want that this dictionary is automatically loaded the next time you start owl, activate »Options, Load this dictionary at next start-up«.
You can also give the path to the dictionary as argument at the command line.
